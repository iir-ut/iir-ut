Information Retrieval (IR) involves the science and engineering of indexing, organizing, searching, and interpreting unstructured or semi-structured data, with a focus on text. The course emphasizes the foundational models that support efficient search and organization, while also touching on performance optimization issues. Additionally, it explores contemporary research challenges and methodologies in the field of Information Retrieval.
